About Yoke

Started in 2022 by business geniuses Michael Childs, Brian Mathews, and Jasper Swensen, Yoke is a platform where hardcore DIYers can get together with people that may need a little help getting things done. Yoke has had a history of making sure you get your yard or sidewalks looking like a million bucks, for a fraction of the cost. Based in the beautiful Cache Valley in northern Utah, Yoke is committed to getting you the help you need, wherever you are.
